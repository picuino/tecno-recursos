Cookies policy¶

What are cookies and what are they used for?¶

Cookies are small data files that are downloaded to your computer, tablet, smartphone or any other device when accessing certain web pages. Cookies allow a web page, among other things, to store and retrieve information about the browsing habits of a user or their equipment and, depending on the information they contain and the way in which you use your equipment, they can be used to recognize to user. The user's browser stores cookies on the hard drive or permanent memory, occupying minimal memory space. In no case can cookies damage or harm your device. Most browsers accept the use of cookies by default.

What types of cookies does this Website use?¶

Technical registration cookies:
Registration cookies are created when you register or log in to one of our websites. The content of these cookies include a user ID automatically assigned by the website software and an anonymous session ID. Cookies are used to keep the user session open and to allow the user to know the topics that they have already read in order to optimize their browsing experience. Even if you close the browser, you will remain identified unless you disconnect or close the session. This information is not used for any other purpose.
Other technical cookies:
These cookies allow the user to be remembered in order to improve their browsing experience. They store service settings so that you do not have to reconfigure them each time you visit us. These cookies are used to remember that you have accepted our cookie policy. Once you accept the use of our cookies, the acceptance banner does not appear on other pages or on subsequent visits.
Analytical cookies:

These cookies collect information from your browsing on our Website in a totally anonymous way. With these cookies you can count the number of visitors to each page, the most viewed content or the most used links. It is possible to know if the user who is accessing is new or repeat visit and what is his location. This information helps us to improve navigation and provide a better service.

This website does not use Google Analytics services for analytical cookies, but rather an open source application managed directly by the owner of the website.
